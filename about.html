About 12:03 Perfume

Founded on the idea that scent is a form of self-expression, 12:03 Perfume creates timeless fragrances that capture your most beautiful moments. Each bottle is crafted with passion, precision, and a deep love for individuality.

Our mission is to empower people through the art of scent. We believe that perfume is more than a fragrance—it's a reflection of who you are, your memories, and your emotions.

Our expert perfumers carefully blend natural essences and modern notes to create scents that linger in the air, leaving a subtle yet unforgettable impression.

12:03 Perfume isn’t just a brand—it’s an experience. Whether you wear it for confidence, comfort, or nostalgia, our creations are designed to resonate deeply with your personality.

Discover the elegance of every second, and let your fragrance tell your story—because every moment matters at 12:03.
